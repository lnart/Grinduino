Easy to Build

Grinduino was designed to be easy to build for anyone who is willing to buy the necessary material

Level Up Your Hand Grinder

The Grinduino Project equips your Hand Grinder with features that usually come at a high price point. You will get, Dosing Control with milligrams of accuracy, speed control in RPM, fully automated Grinding experience

Open Source

The Project is open source, that means it's totally free to use and if you want to contribute to the community and the project you can also do that!
